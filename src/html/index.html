El Refugio

El Refugio is a family-oriented vacation destination located on Lago Espolon and focused on togetherness
Ever since our first visit to Chile in 2005, we've been entranced with the spectacular scenery, fresh air, and dramatic terrain. Later we moved here because we wanted to give our children the privelege of growing up this amazin environment. Now, we've decided to share the magic. El Refugio is the place we would have loved to visit as a family.
